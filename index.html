UX Design Process

I begin my design projects by defining and scoping the presented problem, get to know my users though interview and survey, then strategically map out solutions through series of user flows, wireframes and prototypes. I test the usability of my designs to ensure a delightful user experience.

UI & Visual Design

Simplicity and quality are my design focus. I create a brand identity that shapes the user interface of each of my products. Through finding the right color palette, typography and tone of voice, I turn any low-fidelity UX wireframes into beautiful and intuitive prototypes.
